About Me

My name is Oluwaseun Lawal and I am an entry-level frontend developer with less than a year experience. I developed this website with my knowledge of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. While still trying to be grounded in JavaScript, I've taken up ReactJS. Also, I am working on some other web projects which I plan continuing and finishing with ReactJS. I will be adding these web projects to my portfolio as soon as I'm done with them.

I am also a self-motivated technical writer with one year experience - though I initially started out as a technical content writer. I have written and can write technical reports, technical documentation, maintenance manuals, and user guides. I have published two of my technical articles on dev.to (a large online community of web developers and software developers).

Last but not the least, I am a service-focused content writer with 3+ years of experience, who specialises in writing web contents and blog posts in multi-niches. The niches I have written and can write in are technology, lifestyle, DIY, health, and entertainment. Some of my works are available in my portfolio.
