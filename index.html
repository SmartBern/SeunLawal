About Me

My name is Oluwaseun Lawal and I am an entry-level frontend developer with less than a year experience. I developed this portfolio website with my knowledge of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. After gaining a fairly good understanding of JavaScript, I'm now learning ReactJS. Currently, I'm working on some other web projects which I'll be adding to my portfolio as soon as I'm done with them.

I am also a self-motivated technical writer with more than one year experience - though I initially started out as a technical content writer. I've written and can write technical reports and documentation, maintenance manuals, and user guides. Two of my technical articles has been published on dev.to (a large online community for software developers) and I'm currently working on another technical article.

Last but not the least, I am a service-focused content writer with 3+ years of experience, who specialises in writing web contents and blog posts in multi-niches. The niches I have written and can write in are technology, lifestyle, DIY, health, and entertainment. Some of my works are available in my portfolio.
